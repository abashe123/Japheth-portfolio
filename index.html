About Me

Hello! My name is Japheth, and I enjoy building systems that solve real-world problems through technology. My journey into software development started with a strong curiosity for how systems work, which led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. Along the way, I developed a passion for web and mobile application development, especially when I began building practical solutions like healthcare management systems and e-commerce platforms.

Fast-forward to today, I've worked on several real-world projects, including a medical test management system, an e-commerce web application, and an IoT-based road condition monitoring system. These experiences have strengthened my ability to design and develop scalable, user-focused applications. My current focus is on building efficient and user-friendly web applications using Laravel, while also expanding my expertise in mobile and embedded systems as part of my Master's studies.

I'm also passionate about sharing knowledge and have experience teaching and guiding students in technology-related subjects, helping them build confidence and practical skills. Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:
